About Us

Meet Our CEO

Welcome to Queen City Marina, our CEO is Jake Boyles. Jake has always been fascinated with the boating industry and being on the water. At the age of 15 he bought his first boat and its been an amazing ride ever since. Jake bought the marina 5 years ago and has worked to turn it around into a place where people all over the country come to buy and sell boats and to also experience the greatness that is Brookville Lake. Jake currently lives in Brookville and is very passionate about the boating community around town. You can contact Jake at Jake @ queencitymarina.com.
